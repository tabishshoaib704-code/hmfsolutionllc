Revenue Cycle Management

At HMF Solutions LLC we provide a wide range of healthcare back-office services. From appointment scheduling, scribing, medical coding, prior authorizations, charge entry and payment posting to AR/Follow UP, we have you covered. Our experienced revenue cycle management team is here to make sure that your operations are seamless, and your reimbursements are on an upwards trajectory.

🏥

Medical Coding

We have a team of Certified and Experienced Professional Coders to provide you with complete medical coding services along with updated coding guidelines. With command over accurate use of ICD, CPT Codes and modifiers our coding team makes sure that you are billing with maximum accuracy to avoid and minimize chances for payers to request additional documentation or deny claims for coding related issues.

🔍

Claim Scrubbing

Our Billing team is expert and trained to scrub the claims for any errors to make sure clean claim submission. This helps in avoiding unnecessary rejections and denials. Our clean claim submission accuracy is over 95%. Any rejections are corrected and resubmitted on the same day.

✅

Prior Authorizations

Our prior authorization team specializes in the process for individual physicians, hospitals, laboratories and Surgery Centers. We provide services from initiation of prior authorization through to the approvals within a quick timeframe.

Eligibility & Benefit Verification

Insurance verification is a time-intensive process and requires minute attention to detail. Our team can ensure you accurate insurance verification services. Benefit Verification is equally important as it provides you an upfront profit estimation.

A/R Follow Up

Our well versed and multispecialty trained team can take on AR of any facility and any size and make a difference in a very short time to streamline the cashflows and get those old aged claims paid in a quick time frame.

Denial Management

We don't just have AR executives to fix denials — we have the best RCM experts that not only resolve the denials, but go an extra mile to find the root cause and eliminate it once and for all.

Patient Collections

Studies show that patient collection costs medical practices one-third of their revenue. We make sure that you're not one of them. Patients receive automated due balance statements via email or text reminders periodically to pay their due balance.

Answering Services
